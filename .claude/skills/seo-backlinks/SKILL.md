# Backlink Outreach Generator

## Step 1: Identify Link-Worthy Pages
Our best pages for earning backlinks:
- /blog/ articles (educational content)
- /services/vrv-vrf-commissioning (technical resource)
- /services/ (comprehensive service overview)
- Any data-driven or comparison articles in blog

## Step 2: Find Prospects
Search for these types of sites relevant to AXIMUS:

### High Priority
- HVAC industry publications and blogs
- California commercial real estate associations
- Facility management websites (BOMA, IFMA chapters)
- Energy efficiency publications (California focus)
- Commercial building operations resources

### Medium Priority
- Local California business directories
- Orange County Chamber of Commerce type sites
- General contractor resource sites
- Property management company blogs

### For Each Prospect Evaluate:
- Relevance to VRF/HVAC or commercial real estate (1-10)
- Estimated site authority (well-known publication = high)
- Link opportunity type: guest post / resource page / directory / mention
- Contact information available?

## Step 3: Generate Outreach Emails
For each prospect create a personalized email:

**Subject line:** Specific, not generic. Reference their content.
**Opening:** Mention something specific about their site or a recent article.
**Value proposition:** What unique expertise or content we can offer.
**Link request:** Natural, not pushy. Frame as adding value to their readers.
**CTA:** One clear next step.

Email length: 150-200 words maximum. Short and specific wins.

## Step 4: Save Results
Save all prospects and emails to: src/content/seo-outreach-tracker.md

Format:
| # | Site | URL | Type | Our page to link | Status | Date |
|---|------|-----|------|-----------------|--------|------|
| 1 | ... | ... | Guest post | /blog/... | Draft | ... |

## IMPORTANT RULES
- These are TEMPLATES only. User sends emails manually, never automatically.
- Always get user approval before finalizing any email.
- Never send bulk or automated outreach — Google penalizes this.
- Focus on quality over quantity: 5 relevant links beat 50 irrelevant ones.
- Update tracker after each outreach attempt and response.
